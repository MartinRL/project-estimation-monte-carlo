Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

The Short Version: Your project data stays on your device. We use Google Analytics to understand how people use the site, and Google AdSense to show ads. That's it.

Your Project Data is Private

All estimation calculations happen in your browser. The story counts, throughput data, and risk assessments you enter never leave your device. We cannot see or access any of your project information.

Analytics and Advertising

Google Analytics - We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use this tool (pages visited, features used, etc.). This helps us improve the site. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect standard web log information like your browser type, pages visited, and visit duration.

Google AdSense - We display ads from Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to show you relevant ads based on your browsing history.

Your Choices

  • Opt out of Google Analytics: Install the opt-out browser add-on
  • Control ad personalization: Google Ads Settings
  • Learn about Google's privacy practices: Google Privacy Policy

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Check the effective date at the top to see when it was last updated.
